## Models With Explicit Credit Pricing

### Video (per call)

- **Sora 2 Pub (text-to-video, image-to-video):** 20 credits/call  
- **Sora 2 VIP (text-to-video, image-to-video):** 100 credits/call

## Notes

1. **积分 (Credits/Points)** are the main billing unit; pricing for packs is account-gated.
2. **Per-second models** (Veo, Grok Imagine Video, Kling O3, etc.) charge by video duration.
3. **Per-call models** (Sora 2 Pub/VIP, Sora 2 角色创建) charge a fixed amount per request.
4. Many **Image** and **Audio** models do not show credits on the public models list; check individual model pages.
5. **Kling O3** variants (Standard/Pro) use per-second billing for 3–15s videos; exact rates are on model detail pages.
